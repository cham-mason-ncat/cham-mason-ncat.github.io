About our Company

The Naor brand started with an idea, an idea for affordable clothing for college students that still looks good and has style. We focus on making clothing that doesn't hurt our buyers pockets and allows customers to still step out the dorm, apartment, and etc and still look good. we strive to give our customers good quality products and quality customer service. All the products are hand-crafted. The lead designer takes time to make every product worth while and quality.
